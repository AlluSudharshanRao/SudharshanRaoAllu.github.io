About

I'm Sudharshan Rao Allu, a Master's student in Computer Engineering at NYU Tandon. I build backend systems and machine learning pipelines—reliable services with Python, REST APIs, Docker/Kubernetes, and production-style ML workflows from experiment to deployment.

I've shipped projects across the stack: a RAG document assistant (Streamlit, ChromaDB, LangChain) with multi-format uploads and hybrid retrieval, deployed on Streamlit Cloud; Django backends for job portals (SMTP, MySQL) and JWT-based auth APIs; and end-to-end ML pipelines with MLflow/DVC and FastAPI. I also built a CNN-based intrusion detection system for IoV (99.9% F1 on Car-Hacking and CICIDS2017) and contributed to predictive maintenance models (LSTM, FFT features) during my IntelliThink internship.

My research includes SDN load balancing (switch-migration algorithms, 33.6% resource utilization and 37.3% load imbalance improvement) and medical imaging (Alzheimer's detection with CNNs/Transformers, 99.53% accuracy). I've worked at NYU Secure Systems Lab (Lind, WebAssembly/RustPOSIX), NIT Sikkim, Zessta, and IntelliThink.
